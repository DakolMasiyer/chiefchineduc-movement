About the Candidate

A Builder Before He Became a Candidate

Chief Engr Chinedu Chukwuonye is an accomplished entrepreneur, infrastructure developer, and Chairman of Coduc Nigeria Limited — with a strong track record of building projects, creating jobs, and supporting communities across Imo State.

A proud son of Isiala Mbano, he has consistently demonstrated commitment to grassroots development through youth empowerment and community-focused initiatives. His private sector results are now his campaign platform.

Campaign Agenda

Our 7-Point Agenda

Seven specific commitments — each with a named signature project, a timeline, and a visible outcome. Not promises. Plans.

🛣️

Infrastructure First

The Okigwe Road Restoration Bill — introduced within 90 days of swearing-in. Rural roads, market access roads, federal advocacy.

⚡

Power & Energy Access

The Okigwe Electrification Initiative — community solar pilot before election day. No community left in darkness.

💼

Jobs & Local Economy

The Okigwe Trades & Skills Fund — 500 youth trained before election day, with names and faces documented.

🛡️

Security & Safety

The Okigwe Community Watch Alliance — structured, funded, and active across all LGAs. Safe communities, safe movement.

💧

Water Access

Clean Water for Every Ward — 10 boreholes commissioned before election day. Visible, photographable, verifiable.

🏥

Healthcare Access

The Okigwe Health Equity Bill — within 100 days in Senate. No one should travel miles to survive.

🎓

Youth & Education

Stay Home, Build Okigwe Programme — scholarships, digital hubs, apprenticeship matching. Keep our future at home.
