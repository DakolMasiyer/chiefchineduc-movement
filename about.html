Full Biography

The Man Behind the Movement

Chief Chinedu Chukwuonye is an accomplished entrepreneur, infrastructure developer, and Chairman of Coduc Nigeria Limited, with a strong track record of building projects, creating jobs, and supporting communities across Imo State.

A proud son of Isiala Mbano, he has consistently demonstrated a commitment to grassroots development through youth empowerment and community-focused initiatives. Driven by a desire to translate private sector results into public service impact, he is seeking to represent Okigwe Zone in the Senate.

His ambition is rooted in restoring dignity, delivering real development, and ensuring that the voices and needs of his people are effectively represented. He does not arrive at this campaign as a newcomer to the work — he arrives having already started it.

"This is not just a political ambition. It is a responsibility. I have seen what is possible when leadership is focused on delivery. Now is the time to bring that same approach to public service."

— CHIEF ENGR CHINEDU CHUKWUONYE
